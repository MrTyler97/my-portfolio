Research at Eastern Michigan University

Department of Chemistry Research

Refactoring a pipeline for the Department of Chemistry gave me a deeper understanding of core Software Engineering principles, including Agile methodologies, version control, debugging, technical communication, and team collaboration. This project was overseen by my professor Dr. Ourania Spantidi.

Autonomous Drone Research

This research project focused on developing an autonomous drone capable of navigating mazes and collecting data from various sensors. I wrote Python scripts to gather sensor data and, in collaboration with my teammate, used nested loops and conditional logic to fine-tune the calibration.
